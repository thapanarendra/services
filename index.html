My Journey

1980s-1990s
Army Kid Foundation
Born in Mhow (Army Cantt), Madhya Pradesh to a Gorkha Rifles officer from Nepal. Growing up as an Army kid instilled discipline and adaptability, with schooling across Shimla, Shillong, and Dehradun. This nomadic lifestyle taught me resilience and the ability to thrive in diverse environments.
Late 1990s
The IT Revolution Begins
Completed Bachelor of Commerce from Shillong during the dawn of the internet era. In my final year, I discovered computers and programming languages. Joined NIIT - a top-tier computer learning institute - marking my first step into the technology world that would define my career.
2002-2004
The Jetking Discovery
A pivotal moment at Jetking Institute in Indore. Their famous billboard showing a software engineer with no fish vs. a hardware/network professional with a basket full of fish convinced me to pursue Hardware and Networking. This 2.5-year diploma became the foundation of my technical expertise.
2005
First Steps as IT Engineer
Landed my first job as Hardware and Network Engineer at Networking Information Services. Managed IT infrastructure for manufacturing industries in Pithampur, learning hands-on about computers, networks, security, and operating systems. This real-world experience was invaluable.
2005-2007
The Pune Chapter Begins
Moved to Pune to explore new opportunities. Pursued Red Hat Enterprise Linux certification as Linux was gaining popularity. This decision led to joining Symantec (Veritas) as a Quality Assurance Engineer - my entry into the software industry, transitioning from commerce to infrastructure to software.
2007-2016
The Veritas Decade
10 transformative years at Veritas Technologies. Started as Associate Software Engineer, rose to Principal Software Engineer. Specialized in Unix products (AIX, HP-UX), Veritas Cluster Server, and storage solutions. Learned SDLC, QA, development, leadership, and built lasting professional relationships. The culture and learning at Veritas shaped my career foundation.
2016-Present
GS Lab/Neurealm Era
Joined GS Lab (now Neurealm) seeking vertical growth and services industry experience. Perfect timing with the cloud revolution! Led multiple cloud projects, POCs on AWS and OpenStack. Mastered project management, client relations, business development, and leadership. Witnessed company's PE acquisition by Kedara and rebranding to Neurealm in June 2025.
2015-2022
Cloud Mastery Journey
Became an early cloud advocate, working on multi-cloud projects, cloud orchestration, and FinOps. Earned certifications in AWS, Azure, and GCP. Also expanded into networking and security domains, continuously evolving with technology trends. Added ITIL and Scrum Master certifications to stay current with industry practices.
November 2022-Present
The AI Revolution
ChatGPT's launch changed everything! Witnessed the birth of the AI era firsthand. What seemed like Hollywood sci-fi became reality. Quickly adapted to GenAI, prompt engineering, and AI models. Now championing AI adoption across teams and clients, just as I did with cloud technology. The future is AI, and we're living through this incredible transformation.
In the AI era, it's not about replacing humans, but about humans evolving with AI to create unprecedented possibilities.
- Narendra Thapa
