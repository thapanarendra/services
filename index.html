My Journey: From Army Kid to Tech Leader

1980s-1990s
🏕️ Army Kid Foundation
Born in Mhow Army Cantonment, Madhya Pradesh to a Gorkha Rifles officer from Nepal. Growing up as an Army kid instilled discipline and adaptability.
🎯 Character Building: Constant relocations across Shimla, Shillong, Indore, and Dehradun taught me to adapt quickly and embrace change as opportunity.
The discipline of army life and witnessing my father's dedication to the nation shaped my core principles that guide me even today.
Late 1990s
💻 The Digital Awakening
Completing Bachelor of Commerce in Shillong during the dawn of the internet revolution. In my final year, I discovered computers and programming languages.
🔥 The Spark: Learning C programming and discovering the magic of making machines think ignited a passion that would define my entire career path.
Joining NIIT wasn't just education - it was stepping into a future I could barely imagine, where technology would transform the world.
2002-2004
🎯 The Jetking Epiphany
A life-changing moment at Jetking Institute in Indore. Their famous billboard showed contrasting futures: software vs. hardware/networking professionals.
💡 Strategic Decision: Chose the road less traveled in India - Hardware and Networking over software development, which proved to be my competitive advantage.
Those 2.5 years weren't just about learning technology; they were about understanding how digital infrastructure powers the modern world.
2005
🔧 First Steps in the Real World
My debut as Hardware and Network Engineer at Networking Information Services, managing IT infrastructure for manufacturing industries in Pithampur.
🎓 Hands-on University: Learning to troubleshoot servers at 2 AM, managing network outages, understanding that technology serves business.
Every problem solved was a victory, every system restored was a lesson in resilience and the critical role technology plays in business continuity.
2005-2007
🚀 The Pune Adventure Begins
Moving to Pune - India's IT capital was both thrilling and terrifying. Pursued Red Hat Enterprise Linux certification as open-source was gaining momentum.
🔮 Technology Foresight: Betting on Linux when Windows dominated enterprise was a calculated risk that opened doors to the software industry.
Landing at Symantec (Veritas) as QA Engineer marked my transition from commerce to infrastructure to software professional - continuous reinvention.
2007-2016
🏛️ The Veritas Transformation Decade
10 extraordinary years at Veritas Technologies - from Associate to Principal Software Engineer. Specialized in Unix systems, Veritas Cluster Server, and enterprise storage.
🎯 Leadership Evolution: Mastered SDLC, QA processes, developed leadership skills, and built relationships that last a lifetime. Veritas was my technology university.
The culture of excellence, innovation, and customer obsession shaped my professional DNA and taught me that great technology serves great purposes.
2016-Present
☁️ The Cloud Revolution Era
Joining GS Lab (now Neurealm) for vertical growth coincided perfectly with the cloud revolution! Led cloud projects, AWS and OpenStack POCs.
🌟 Business Acumen: Mastered project management, client relations, business development. Witnessed company's PE acquisition by Kedara and rebranding to Neurealm.
From technical expert to business leader - learning that technology success is measured by business impact and customer satisfaction.
2015-2022
🌩️ Cloud Mastery and Multi-Domain Excellence
Became an early cloud evangelist, working on multi-cloud architectures, cloud orchestration, and FinOps before they became mainstream.
📚 Continuous Learning: Earned certifications in AWS, Azure, GCP, expanded into networking and security. Added ITIL and Scrum Master certifications.
Realized that in technology, learning never stops - those who adapt and evolve thrive, while those who rest on achievements become obsolete.
November 2022-Present
🤖 The AI Renaissance
ChatGPT's launch on November 30, 2022 didn't just introduce a product - it announced the arrival of the AI age! Science fiction became reality.
🚀 AI Pioneer: Quickly mastered GenAI, prompt engineering, and AI model integration. Now championing AI adoption across teams and clients.
Witnessing the birth of artificial general intelligence feels like watching the dawn of a new era - we're not just using tools, we're collaborating with intelligence.
From an Army kid learning discipline in cantonments to an AI pioneer shaping the future of technology - every step was preparation for this moment where human creativity meets artificial intelligence to solve tomorrow's challenges.
- Narendra Thapa's Journey Philosophy
